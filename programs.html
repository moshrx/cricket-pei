Our Programs

From competitive league play to grassroots youth training, we provide a pathway for every cricketer on the Island.

Professional League

CCL PEI

The Charlottetown Cricket League (CCL) is the premier hard-ball competition in PEI. Featuring multiple clubs across the province, the league showcases high-intensity matches, professional stats tracking via CricHeroes, and a path to provincial selection.

Kids Program

Building the next generation of Island cricketers. Our youth outreach focuses on schools and summer camps, teaching the fundamentals of batting, bowling, and sportsmanship in a safe, fun environment.

Provincial Tournaments

Cricket PEI proudly represents the Island in Atlantic Championships. We host and travel for inter-provincial clashes against Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, and Newfoundland & Labrador.

  • Maritime Inter-Provincial Series
